Local Wealth Fund

Overview

Let your community utilize our local investment fund. Allow every citizen to be a shareholder in hometown equity. Be a stakeholder in what you use everyday.

Details

The Local Wealth Fund works by taking a small percentage of the investments processed on the platform and sets them aside in a non profit entity. Each year, the platform participants (investors, businesses, community organizations) take a vote on what community initiative these funds should be spent on in some way that benefits the local community.

Benefits

  • Invest in your local community and support economic development.
  • Empower local residents to have a say in where the funds are spent.
  • Strengthen the relationship between investors and the local community.
